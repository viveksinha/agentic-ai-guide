Suggested Learning Path
If you only have 2 hours total, here's the optimal order.
  1. 1

    Watch (10 min): Bernard Marr 60-second + 4-minute videos

    Get the core concept locked in. The trip-planning analogy is all you need to explain this to anyone.

  2. 2

    Read (10 min): MIT Sloan — Agentic AI Explained

    Adds credibility and depth to what the videos covered. Clean, no jargon.

  3. 3

    Read (10 min): Allianz Case Study

    The 29 days → 3.5 days number is the best business case you'll find in insurance. Share this widely.

  4. 4

    Try (15 min): Open claude.ai and test the annuity agent

    See the How-To Guide for the system prompt. Paste it into a Claude Project and test it yourself — no coding needed.

  5. 5

    Explore (30 min): Copilot Studio pre-built agents

    Go to copilotstudio.microsoft.com and browse the pre-built templates. Pick one relevant to your team and deploy it to Teams.
